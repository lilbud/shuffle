We've met Martin Ekman before. [His cover]({{< relref "2021-08-12-cover-me-martin-ekman-and-daniel-falcone-borg-the-river.md" >}}) of "[The River]({{< relref "2021-03-04-roll-of-the-dice-the-river.md" >}})" with Daniel Falcone Borg was one of my personal favorites from our *Cover Me* spotlights last year

Martin's quite the cover artist on his own, though. For the past year or so, he's been on a mission to arrange and record 100 of his favorite songs, all performed entirely by himself on multiple instruments.

This week's entry (#63) was another of Bruce's songs, and as usual, it's a wonderful interpretation.

{{< youtube 0M2JYth-b04 >}}

Martin's version of "[Secret Garden]({{< relref "2019-05-30-roll-of-the-dice-secret-garden.md" >}})" starts out in a relatively faithful solo acoustic guitar arrangement, but by the end of the first verse he takes liberties with the melody and gently layers on instrumentation (including a subtle accordion that adds a nostalgic note to the song). The result is a beautiful and nuanced recording.

For more of Marin's music, check out his YouTube page [here](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCE7YEbvxRk_GsKWW1T4qEmg).
