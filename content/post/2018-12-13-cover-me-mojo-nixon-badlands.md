Thanks to Jason Luttrull for bringing this one to my attention!

I’m a big fan of Mojo Nixon’s cover of “[The Big Payback]({{< relref "2018-11-11-roll-of-the-dice-the-big-payback.md" >}})” from his first album with Skid Roper, and I’ve long wished he’d take a run at another song from Bruce’s catalog.

Little did I know he’d already done so back in 1997–sort of.

Turns out that while rehearsing for his *Sock Ray Blue* album in the studio, his rehearsal sessions included a rough cut of “[Badlands]({{< relref "2016-01-20-roll-of-the-dice-badlands.md" >}}),” The song never made it on the album, but it’s a bold, fresh (but still just as fierce) arrangement that deserves to be heard.
