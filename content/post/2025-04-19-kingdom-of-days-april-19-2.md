***New for 2025:** New entry for 1975 and 1980; video for 2014*<br>

**1974:** Bruce and the not quite E Street Band play a poorly advertised (and therefore poorly attended) show at the New Jersey State Theater in New Brunswick. In attendance tonight: Penn Jillette.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/04/1974-04-19.jpg.webp)

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/04/1974-0419.jpg.webp)

**1975:** Bruce works on “Thunder Road” in the studio at the Record Plant in New York City.<br>

**1980:** Bruce finalizes the mix for “[Ramrod]({{< relref "2022-09-10-roll-of-the-dice-ramrod.md" >}})” at Clover Studios in Los Angeles.<br>

{{< youtube BEhmiGQvjOY >}}

**1981:** One night after debuting “[Can’t Help Falling in Love,]({{< relref "1976-01-21-cover-me-cant-help-falling-in-love.md" >}}/)” Bruce opens his second show in Paris by premiering his cover/adaptation of “Follow That Dream.” The encores include another world premiere: Bruce’s first cover of “[Sweet Soul Music]({{< relref "1976-01-20-cover-me-sweet-soul-music.md" >}}/).”<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/04/1981-04-18-1.jpg.webp)

**1985:** Bruce’s tour of Japan moves from Tokyo to Kyoto for a night at Furitsu Taikukan.<br>

**2008:** Bruce reschedules a scheduled show in Orlando due to the passing of Danny Federici.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2024/04/The_Miami_Herald_Fri__Apr_18__2008_-scaled.jpg.webp)

**2014:** Record Store Day is a busy one: Bruce releases a new EP, [*American Beauty*]({{< relref "2022-02-05-album-companion-american-beauty.md" >}}), performs [the title track]({{< relref "2019-02-21-roll-of-the-dice-american-beauty.md" >}}) at soundcheck in Charlotte, and films it for release a few days later. That night, Bruce stuns the audience by opening with only the second-ever performance of “[Iceman]({{< relref "2022-02-01-roll-of-the-dice-iceman.md" >}})” and a cover-filled set that included “[Louie Louie]({{< relref "2020-03-20-cover-me-louie-louie.md" >}}),” “[Mustang Sally,]({{< relref "2020-07-15-matr-bruce-springsteen-and-just-about-everybody-mustang-sally.md" >}})” and “[Brown-Eyed Girl]({{< relref "2018-09-20-cover-me-brown-eyed-girl.md" >}}).” One of my favorite shows. For highlights, see my [*Where the Band Was* report]({{< relref "2018-04-19-where-the-band-was-charlotte-april-19-2014.md" >}}) from this show.<br>
