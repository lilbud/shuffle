**1972:** Upon returning home from his aborted relocation to California, Bruce reunites with his eponymous band (now called The Sundance Blues Band) at The Captain’s Garter in Neptune, New Jersey.<br>

**1973**: Bruce and the band play their final of four nights at My Father’s Place on Long Island.<br>

**1981:** Night Two in Toronto on the River Tour. Listen to the show below.<br>

**1987**: At the second Rock and Roll Hall of Fame ceremonies, Bruce inducts Roy Orbison and performs with him on “[Oh, Pretty Woman]({{< relref "2019-08-25-matr-roy-orbison-and-bruce-springsteen-oh-pretty-woman.md" >}}).”<br>

**1996:** The television news show *60 Minutes* profiles Bruce and his solo acoustic tour.<br>

{{< videopress id=lH6SiNVz >}}

**2006:** Bruce assembles the Seeger Sessions band for a final recording session, capturing “[Eyes on the Prize]({{< relref "2006-01-10-cover-me-eyes-on-the-prize.md" >}}),” “[Jacob’s Ladder]({{< relref "2006-01-08-rotd-cover-me-jacobs-ladder.md" >}}),” “[Buffalo Gals]({{< relref "2006-01-15-cover-me-roll-of-the-dice-buffalo-gals.md" >}}),” “[How Can I Keep From Singing]({{< relref "2006-01-16-cover-me-how-can-i-keep-from-singing.md" >}})” “[Bring ’em Home]({{< relref "2006-01-18-rotd-cover-me-bring-em-home.md" >}})” and “[American Land]({{< relref "2016-01-22-roll-of-the-dice-american-land.md" >}}).” Of these, only “American Land” remains unreleased from this session. (Bruce re-recorded it for *Wrecking Ball*.)<br>
