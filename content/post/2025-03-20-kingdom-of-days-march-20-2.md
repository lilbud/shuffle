***New for 2025:** Audio for 2008; video for 2013 and 2023*<br>

**1974:** Bruce and his band play two shows at their third night at Gertie's in Dallas.<br>

**1977:** "Juicy Brucey" and the E Street Band bring the Lawsuit Tour to Providence College.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/03/1977-0320.jpg.webp)

**1981:** A scheduled show in London is postponed due to Bruce's exhaustion from the first two legs of the River Tour.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/03/1981-0318.jpg.webp)

**1985:** A New Zealand news station covers the anticipation over the Australian leg of the Born in the U.S.A. Tour.<br>

**2000:** The day after the E Street Band plays a reunion show in New Orleans, Bruce and Patti visit Emmylou Harris in her producer's New Orleans studio, where they contribute backing vocals for Emmylou's song "[Tragedy]({{< relref "2018-01-17-meeting-across-the-river-emmylou-harris-bruce-springsteen-patti-scialfa.md" >}})."<br>

{{< youtube jf7BSf5ezUY >}}

**2003:** On the eve of the Iraq War, Bruce opens the Australia leg of The Rising Tour with an acoustic "[Born in the U.S.A.]({{< relref "1984-01-03-roll-of-the-dice-born-in-the-u-s-a.md" >}})" at the Telstra Dome in Melbourne. "[War]({{< relref "2016-01-02-cover-me-war.md" >}})" also receives a rare outing.<br>

{{< videopress id=uZuuks3s >}}

**2006:** Bruce rehearses with The Sessions Band at the Paramount Theater in Asbury Park.<br>

**2008:** Danny Federici appears on-stage with the E Street Band for the final time at Bruce's Magic show at the Conseco Fieldhouse in Indianapolis, where Bruce and the band tour premiere "[Rendezvous]({{< relref "2021-05-08-roll-of-the-dice-rendezvous.md" >}})." Watch the official, emotional video of "[4th of July, Asbury Park (Sandy)]({{< relref "2019-04-26-roll-of-the-dice-4th-of-july-asbury-park-sandy.md" >}})" (also available on the *Magic Tour Highlights* EP). This show is available as part of Bruce's official archive series–get it [here](https://live.brucespringsteen.net/live-music/0,27552/Bruce-Springsteen-mp3-flac-download-3-20-2008-Conseco-Fieldhouse-Indianapolis-IN.html).<br>

**2013:** Bruce marks the tenth anniversary of the Iraq War at Allphones Arena in Sydney, opening with an acoustic "[Devils & Dust]({{< relref "2022-03-02-roll-of-the-dice-devils-dust.md" >}})" and launching into a full-band "Last to Die."<br>
