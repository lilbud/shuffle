**1974:** After firing drummer Vini Lopez three days earlier, Bruce cancels a show at the University of Toledo so that the band has time to rehearse with new drummer Ernest “Boom” Carter.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/02/1974-02-15.jpg.webp)

**1977:** Bruce and the E Street band return to the U.S. and continue the Lawsuit Tour at Detroit’s Masonic Temple Theater, where “[Detroit Medley]({{< relref "1976-01-02-cover-me-detroit-medley.md" >}}/)” makes its tour premiere.<br>

{{< youtube kiO9Y-V2z8Y >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/02/46881241794_6812cfd98f_o.jpg.webp)

**1981**: Bruce and band take the River Tour to the Civic Center in Lakeland, Florida.<br>

{{< videopress id=XYZd28NA >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/02/1981-02-15b.png.webp)

**1983:** In the studio, Bruce records “[Seven Tears]({{< relref "1983-01-07-roll-of-the-dice-seven-tears.md" >}})” and “The Money We Didn’t Make.” The former circulates among collectors, the latter has not yet surfaced.<br>

{{< youtube 6DNs1enmtNM >}}

**1988:** At the Wonder Bar in Asbury Park, Bruce films footage used in the video for “[One Step Up]({{< relref "1987-01-10-roll-of-the-dice-one-step-up.md" >}}/).”<br>

{{< youtube MkFQHScyti0 >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/02/1988-02-15.png.webp)

**1995:** Bruce joins Melissa Etheridge for her MTV Unplugged performance in Brooklyn. They perform a gorgeous duet of “Thunder Road” together.<br>

{{< youtube 5cXzFhZVMnQ >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2022/02/1995-02-15.jpg.webp)

**1996:** Bruce plays his third solo acoustic show in Germany, this time in Munich.<br>

{{< videopress id=3d7UJujn >}}

**1997:** Moving on to Melbourne, Bruce performs solo at the Palais Theatre, where he plays “[4th of July, Asbury Park (Sandy)]({{< relref "2019-04-26-roll-of-the-dice-4th-of-july-asbury-park-sandy.md" >}})” for the first time in Australia.<br>

{{< videopress id=qmr0Nwtj >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/02/1997-02-15.png.webp)

**2014:** The first of two nights in Melbourne features a surprise guest in the opening numbers: Eddie Vedder joins the E Street Band for “Highway to Hell” and “[Darkness on the Edge of Town]({{< relref "2016-01-07-roll-of-the-dice-darkness-on-the-edge-of-town.md" >}}).” Another surprise shortly follows: a full performance of the *Born in the U.S.A.* album. Also tour premiering tonight: “[Jole Blon,]({{< relref "2019-08-29-matr-gary-u-s-bonds-and-bruce-springsteen-jole-blon.md" >}})” “[My Hometown]({{< relref "1983-01-12-roll-of-the-dice-my-hometown.md" >}}),” “[I’m Goin’ Down]({{< relref "1984-01-11-roll-of-the-dice-im-goin-down.md" >}}),” and “[Factory]({{< relref "2018-10-31-roll-of-the-dice-factory.md" >}}).”<br>
