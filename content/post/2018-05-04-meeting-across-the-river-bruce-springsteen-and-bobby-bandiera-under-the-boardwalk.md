Can anything scream “summer at the Jersey shore” more than Bruce Springsteen singing “Under the Boardwalk?” I don’t think so.

Here’s the first time Bruce played the The Drifters’ 1964 classic, with Bobby Bandiera at The Stone Pony on June 24, 1989.

{{< youtube j0h8kVXEdX0 >}}

“Under the Boardwalk” is a sequel of sorts to “[Up on the Roof]({{< relref "2018-12-15-cover-me-up-on-the-roof.md" >}}),” another gorgeous Drifters song that Bruce has covered a few times (but we’ll cover that another day). Here’s the original, but you almost certainly know it–the opening notes played on bass, gourd, and triangle are instantly recognizable.

{{< youtube EPEqRMVnZNU >}}

Bruce covered “Under the Boardwalk” a few more times over the years. The last time was at a telethon for Hurricane Sandy relief–and it was a team-up as well, this time a bit more star-studded:

{{< youtube lPqDw4VtOJ4 >}}

**Under the Boardwalk**<br>
**First performed:** June 24, 1989 (Asbury Park, NJ)<br>
**Last performed:** November 2, 2012 (New York City, NY)<br>
