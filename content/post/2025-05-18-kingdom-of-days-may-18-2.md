***New for 2025:** Audio for 1999 and 2014; video for 2014 and 2023*<br>

**1966:** Bruce enters a recording studio for the very first time when The Castiles record their first two songs: "[Baby I](../2018-12-20-roll-of-the-dice-baby-i/post.md/)" and "[That's What You Get]({{< relref "2019-11-30-roll-of-the-dice-thats-what-you-get.md" >}})." Both are co-written by Bruce and George Theiss. The former will be officially released on *Chapter & Verse* in 2016; the latter is still unreleased but can be heard below.<br>

{{< youtube _1ET0kehXbg >}}

**1973:** Bruce cancels a scheduled performance at Michigan Technological University to make room for studio time for the Wild and Innocent sessions.<br>

**1988:** Night Two of five in the final U.S. stand for the Tunnel of Love Express Tour. Bruce and the band exit to a "[New York, New York]({{< relref "2021-06-26-cover-me-new-york-new-york.md" >}})" instrumental.<br>

{{< youtube fNUAkKLceYQ >}}

**1997:** Bruce performs a solo acoustic show at the Acropolis in Nice, featuring the tour premiere of "[You Can Look (But You Better Not Touch)]({{< relref "2020-06-14-roll-of-the-dice-you-can-look-but-you-better-not-touch.md" >}})." This show is available as part of Bruce's official archive series–get it [here](https://live.brucespringsteen.net/live-music/0,26013/Bruce-Springsteen-mp3-flac-download-5-18-1997-Palais-des-Congr%C3%A8s-Acropolis-Nice-FR.html).<br>

**2009:** Max returns as the Working on a Dream Tour arrives at the Verizon Center in Washington, DC. The show features three back-to-back tour premieres: "[Little Latin Lupe Lu]({{< relref "1976-01-14-cover-me-little-latin-lupe-lu.md" >}}/)," "Hava Nagila"(!) and "[Blinded by the Light]({{< relref "2011-01-02-roll-of-the-dice-blinded-by-the-light.md" >}})."<br>
