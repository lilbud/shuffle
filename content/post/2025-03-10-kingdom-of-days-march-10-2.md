***New for 2025:** New entry for 1972 ,1978, 2018, and 2024; audio for 1974*<br>

**1967:** The Castiles play the Hullabaloo in Middletown, New Jersey.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/02/1967-0310.jpg.webp)

**1968:** The destined-for-legend Upstage club prepares to open for business in Asbury Park, and the local paper publishes a profile.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/03/1968-03-10.jpg.webp)

**1972:** The Bruce Springsteen Band performs at St. Joseph High School mixer in Metuchen, New Jersey. (The school mangles his name a bit, though.)<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2025/03/medium-2.jpg.webp)

**1974:** Bruce and the still unnamed E Street Band play two shows at Houston's Liberty Hall. The late show features the concert debut of "[The Fever]({{< relref "2020-10-04-roll-of-the-dice-the-fever.md" >}})," "[Gimme That Wine]({{< relref "2019-03-22-cover-me-gimme-that-wine.md" >}})" and "[Angel's Blues]({{< relref "2019-10-05-roll-of-the-dice-angels-blues.md" >}})."<br>

**1977:** An early version of "[Don't Look Back]({{< relref "2020-05-03-roll-of-the-dice-dont-look-back.md" >}})" makes its debut at the Sports Arena in Toledo.<br>

{{< videopress id=ofg8Vn4K >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/03/1977-0310.jpg.webp)

**1978:** Bruce and the band complete their recording of "[Darkness on the Edge of Town]({{< relref "2016-01-07-roll-of-the-dice-darkness-on-the-edge-of-town.md" >}})" at the Record Plant in New York City.<br>

{{< youtube 0LpdEyGhNxM >}}

**1983:** Bruce records "[The Klansman]({{< relref "1983-01-16-roll-of-the-dice-the-klansman.md" >}})" in his home studio. This haunting gem remains unreleased to this day.<br>

{{< youtube 1ewhMge_MXM >}}

**1989:** At Mickey Rourke's Rubber Club in Los Angeles, Bruce joins The Mighty Hornets for "[C.C. Rider]({{< relref "2021-08-29-cover-me-c-c-rider.md" >}})."<br>

**1999:** Bruce and the E Street Band rehearse for their upcoming Reunion Tour at Asbury Park's Convention Hall. News coverage below.<br>

{{< youtube iWe1tQGBS9g >}}

**2000:** Bruce and the reunited E Street Band close out the Florida leg of their Reunion Tour with a second show in Sunrise.<br>
