Australian singer-songwriter Amy Shark made an appearance in Los Angeles-based KIIS-FM's Living Room Concert Series last month, playing a set of her standards and debuting a new song.

To close her set, though, Amy chose a cover–her all-time favorite Springsteen song, "[Secret Garden]({{< relref "2019-05-30-roll-of-the-dice-secret-garden.md" >}})."

Amy's arrangement was a bit more percussive than Bruce's wistful original, with a beautiful trumpet solo to close the song. With her heartfelt vocals soaring above, the full effect is an absolutely gorgeous cover.

Take a listen and enjoy.
