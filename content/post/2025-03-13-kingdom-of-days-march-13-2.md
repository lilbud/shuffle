***New for 2025:** No updates*<br>

**1969:** Child, Bruce's new band, performs at Pandemonium in Wanamassa, New Jersey.<br>

**1971:** Bruce jams with local musicians at an open mic night at The Upstage in Asbury Park.<br>

**1973:** Night Two of seven at Oliver's in Boston.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/03/1973-0316.jpg.webp)

**1975:** Extended studio sessions for *Born to Run* prompt the postponement of a scheduled show in Atlanta.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/03/1975-03-13.jpg.webp)
