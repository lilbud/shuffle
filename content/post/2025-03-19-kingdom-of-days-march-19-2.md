***New for 2025:** Audio for 1977; video for 2009 and 2012; new entry for 2024*<br>

**1974:** Bruce and his band play two shows at their second night at Gertie's in Dallas.<br>

**1977:** The Lawsuit Tour moves north to the Central Maine Youth Center in Lewiston, where writer Stephen King is in attendance.<br>

**1981:** A scheduled show in London is postponed due to Bruce's exhaustion from the first two legs of the River Tour.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/03/1981-0318.jpg.webp)

**1985:** Bruce arrives in Sydney two days before what will be his first-ever show in Australia.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/03/1985-03-19.png.webp)

**1996:** Bruce plays his first-ever show in Northern Ireland, at King's Hall in Belfast. This show is available as part of Bruce's official archive series–[go get it](https://live.brucespringsteen.net/live-music/0,17589/Bruce-Springsteen-mp3-flac-download-3-19-1996-Kings-Hall-Belfast-UK.html).<br>

**1999:** A second public warm-up show for Bruce Springsteen and the E Street Band, their last in the U.S. before the Reunion Tour kicks off. "[Give the Girl a Kiss]({{< relref "2020-06-17-roll-of-the-dice-give-the-girl-a-kiss.md" >}})" makes its live debut, and "The Promise" is played for the first time in 21 years.<br>

**2005:** With the Seeger Sessions Band in his home studio, Bruce records "[Old Dan Tucker]({{< relref "2006-01-02-cover-me-old-dan-tucker.md" >}})," "[Mrs. McGrath]({{< relref "2006-01-04-cover-me-rotd-mrs-mcgrath.md" >}})," "[Erie Canal]({{< relref "2006-01-07-cover-me-erie-canal.md" >}})," "[O Mary Don't You Weep,]({{< relref "2006-01-05-cover-me-o-mary-dont-you-weep.md" >}})" "[John Henry]({{< relref "2006-01-06-cover-me-rotd-john-henry.md" >}})," "[Shenandoah]({{< relref "2006-01-11-cover-me-shenandoah.md" >}})," "[Froggie Went A-courtin]({{< relref "2006-01-14-roll-of-the-dice-cover-me-froggie-went-a-courtin.md" >}})'" and "[Pay Me My Money Down]({{< relref "2006-01-12-roll-of-the-dice-cover-me-pay-me-my-money-down.md" >}})."<br>

{{< youtube tebjshm7f_I >}}

**2009:** Jon Stewart interviews Bruce on The Daily Show, where Bruce performs an acoustic version of "[Working on a Dream]({{< relref "2019-07-04-roll-of-the-dice-working-on-a-dream.md" >}})." Later that evening, Bruce and the E Street Band (with Jay Weinberg filling in for his dad) rehearse at Asbury Park Convention Hall.<br>
