The Mavericks are certainly no stranger to Bruce's catalog–[their cover of "All That Heaven Will Allow"]({{< relref "2019-02-28-cover-me-the-mavericks-all-that-heaven-will-allow.md" >}}) is one of the finer Springsteen covers out there.

Now they're at it again: just a couple of months ago they released their latest album, an all-covers compilation entitled *Play the Hits*.

This time, the band turns their attention to "[Hungry Heart]({{< relref "2022-12-21-roll-of-the-dice-hungry-heart.md" >}})," giving it an infectious country-ska treatment. (I don't know if that's a thing, but it sure sounds like it to me.)

{{< youtube MXzUfPVF08o >}}

It's not surprising to see the band lavishing love an attention on Bruce's songs–they're clearly big fans. And don't just take that from me:

{{< youtube 2ct4WG3v43I >}}
