***New for 2025:** Audio for 1995; new entry for 2024*<br>

**1954:** Carol Dennis, back-up singer for Bruce's 1992-1993 World Tour, is born.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/04/carol.jpg.webp)

**1969:** Child plays the Pandemonium in Wanamassa, New Jersey, part of their month-long residency.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/04/1969-0413.jpg.webp)

**1974:** Bruce plays two shows at Charlie's Place in Cambridge, Massachusetts–his final night of four at that venue.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/04/1974-04-12.jpg.webp)

**1976:** At the Cambria County War Memorial in Johnstown, Pennsylvania, Bruce and the E Street Band play their final full-band "[Blinded by the Light]({{< relref "2011-01-02-roll-of-the-dice-blinded-by-the-light.md" >}})" for 23 years. Promoters are getting Bruce's name right these days, but they're still struggling with the band's.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/04/1976-0412.jpg.webp)

**1980:** Bruce works on mixing and overdubs for several *The River* tracks at Clover Studios in Los Angeles, completing mixes for "[Sherry Darling]({{< relref "2018-07-06-roll-of-the-dice-sherry-darling.md" >}})" and "[Wreck on the Highway.]({{< relref "2022-08-28-roll-of-the-dice-wreck-on-the-highway.md" >}})"<br>

{{< youtube FCS3mewzWSo >}}

{{< youtube D-MthLm7_m0 >}}

**1987:** Bruce, Patti, Max, Roy, Jon Bon Jovi, and John Luraschi join Cats on a Smooth Surface on stage at The Stone Pony, where Bruce plays "[Light of Day]({{< relref "2018-12-06-roll-of-the-dice-light-of-day.md" >}})" for the very first time. Video clips of "[Lucille]({{< relref "1976-01-07-cover-me-lucille.md" >}}/)" and "[Kansas City]({{< relref "2021-01-11-cover-me-kansas-city.md" >}})" have been available on YouTube for a while, but here's a longer, newly uploaded video that includes "[Wooly Bully]({{< relref "2018-03-31-cover-me-woolly-bully.md" >}})" as well.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2022/04/1987-04-12.jpg.webp)

**1988:** The Tunnel of Love Express stops in Houston for the first of two shows at The Summit.<br>

{{< videopress id=qpGF3ADJ >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/04/1988-04-12b.jpg.webp)

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/04/1988-04-12c.jpg.webp)

**1993:** Bruce's second live album is released: *In Concert/M**TV Plugged*. However, the album is only available in Europe, leading American fans to scour record stores for imported copies.<br>

{{< youtube-playlist PLJ3gKh8Ty5pbccXmiIkXNfQ2dUMIu6OVT >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2024/04/The_Jersey_Journal_Fri__Apr_30__1993_-scaled.jpg.webp)

**1995:** Bruce joins Sting, James Taylor, Elton John, Billy Joel, Jon Bon Jovi, Paul Simon, and others at a benefit for the Rainforest Foundation at Carnegie Hall. The set list is fun and Elvis-inspired–besides covers of "[Viva Las Vegas,]({{< relref "2018-12-07-cover-me-viva-las-vegas.md" >}})" "[Burning Love]({{< relref "2018-05-01-cover-me-burning-love.md" >}})" and other classics, Bruce performs "[I'm Turning Into Elvis]({{< relref "2020-03-05-roll-of-the-dice-im-turning-into-elvis.md" >}})" in full for the first time and duets with James Taylor on "[The River]({{< relref "2021-03-04-roll-of-the-dice-the-river.md" >}})."<br>

**2000:** Bruce and the E Street Band play a Reunion Tour show for a low-energy crowd in Nashville. Bruce would still recall it seven years later to friend-of-the-blog Bryan Meng as the "worst crowd of the entire tour." (Nashville would redeem itself on the Magic Tour.)<br>

{{< videopress id=B9bIX3FU >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/04/2000-04-12.jpg.webp)

**2005:** Bruce rehearses for his upcoming solo acoustic tour at the Paramount Theater in Asbury Park.<br>

**2006:** At Asbury Park's Convention Hall, Bruce and the Sessions Band rehearse for their upcoming tour.<br>

**2012:** The Wrecking Ball Tour stops at The Palace of Auburn Hills, where Bruce breaks out tour premieres of "[Incident on 57th Street]({{< relref "2018-12-14-roll-of-the-dice-incident-on-57th-street.md" >}})" and "[Youngstown]({{< relref "2023-05-01-roll-of-the-dice-youngstown.md" >}})."<br>

{{< youtube 9IiTzxRJ6yo >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/04/2012-04-12.jpg.webp)

**2014:** A true seaside show, Bruce's concert in Virginia Beach opens with a one-off cover of "[May I]({{< relref "2019-10-01-cover-me-may-i.md" >}})," and features rare performances of "[Seaside Bar Song]({{< relref "2022-10-24-roll-of-the-dice-seaside-bar-song.md" >}})" and "[Jersey Girl]({{< relref "1976-02-07-cover-me-roll-of-the-dice-jersey-girl.md" >}}/)" along with the tour premieres of "[The Wall]({{< relref "2024-09-04-roll-of-the-dice-the-wall.md" >}})" and "[Talk to Me]({{< relref "2019-02-26-roll-of-the-dice-talk-to-me.md" >}})."<br>

{{< youtube YMBnU1bYsDc >}}

{{< youtube Aa71oVuQcs4 >}}

**2016:** Columbus gets a rare performance of "[I Wanna Be With You]({{< relref "2018-01-14-roll-of-the-dice-i-wanna-be-with-you.md" >}})" when the River Tour stops for a night at the Schottenstein Center.<br>

{{< youtube E7KtVbpNmiE >}}

**2018:** *Springsteen on Broadway* continues its theatrical run at the Walter Kerr Theater in New York City.<br>

**2024:** Bruce and the E Street Band tour premiere "[Seeds]({{< relref "2020-10-10-roll-of-the-dice-seeds.md" >}})" at Mohegan Sun Arena in Uncasville.<br>
