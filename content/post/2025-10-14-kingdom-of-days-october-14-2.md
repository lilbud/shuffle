***New for 2025:** Audio for 1977*<br>

**1972:** Bruce runs a classified ad in the Asbury Park Press seeking a "together and ready to travel" pianist and country violinist. Let's hope he finds at least one of them. Maybe David Sancious is still available?<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/10/1972-10-14.png.webp)

**1977:** At Atlantic Studios in New York City, Bruce records "[Talk to Me]({{< relref "2019-02-26-roll-of-the-dice-talk-to-me.md" >}})," "[Hearts of Stone]({{< relref "2022-09-07-roll-of-the-dice-hearts-of-stone.md" >}})," "[City of Night]({{< relref "2020-06-28-roll-of-the-dice-city-of-night.md" >}})," and "[Wrong Side of the Street]({{< relref "2019-11-06-roll-of-the-dice-wrong-side-of-the-street.md" >}})"– all of which will go unreleased for decades–plus "[The Ballad]({{< relref "2019-12-29-roll-of-the-dice-the-ballad.md" >}})," which is still unreleased to this day.<br>

{{< youtube rtI5TxfhhUU >}}

**1980:** Bruce calls an out-of-left-field audible at his River Tour stop at Milwaukee's Mecca Arena, resulting in his first public performance of "[In the Midnight Hour]({{< relref "2020-12-31-matr-wilson-pickett-and-bruce-springsteen-in-the-midnight-hour.md" >}})."<br>

{{< youtube BsPe6q9jYF0 >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/10/1980-10-14.png.webp)

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2022/10/1980-10-14.jpg.webp)

**1988:** Bruce plays his first-ever show in Argentina (although half of those in attendance were from Chile) when the Human Rights Now! Tour stops in Mendoza.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/10/1988-10-14.png.webp)

**2000:** Bruce jams with The Gotham Playboys (and future Sessions Band bandmates), who are once again the entertainment at Bruce's belated birthday party at his house in Colts Neck.<br>

**2002:** The first European leg of the Rising Tour kicks off in Paris, where Elliot Murphy makes a guest appearance for "Born to Run."<br>

**2007:** The Magic Tour heads north to Canada, starting with a show at ScotiaBank Place in Ottawa, where Win Butler and Regine Chassagne of Arcade Fire guest star for the tour premiere of "[State Trooper]({{< relref "2019-12-22-roll-of-the-dice-state-trooper.md" >}})" and Bruce's only performance of "[Keep the Car Running]({{< relref "2020-04-01-matr-bruce-springsteen-and-arcade-fire-keep-the-car-running.md" >}})" to date. Also tour premiering at this show: "[Tougher Than the Rest]({{< relref "1987-01-02-roll-of-the-dice-tougher-than-the-rest.md" >}}/)" and "[Backstreets]({{< relref "2022-05-14-roll-of-the-dice-backstreets.md" >}})," performed back to back.<br>

**2009:** Night Two of the final four shows at the Philadelphia Spectrum opens with a rare "[Thundercrack]({{< relref "2021-09-24-roll-of-the-dice-thundercrack.md" >}})" and features the only performance to date of "[What Love Can Do]({{< relref "2018-11-25-roll-of-the-dice-what-love-can-do.md" >}})" along with a full album performance of *[Darkness on the Edge of Town]({{< relref "2022-07-05-album-companion-darkness-on-the-edge-of-town.md" >}})*. This show is available to stream or purchase [here](https://www.nugs.net/live-download-of-bruce-springsteen-wachovia-spectrum-philadelphia-pa-10-14-2009-mp3-flac-or-online-music-streaming/35337.html).<br>

**2017:** Bruce can't seem to decide on the order of "[Long Walk Home]({{< relref "2020-03-21-roll-of-the-dice-long-walk-home.md" >}})" and "[The Rising]({{< relref "2016-01-18-roll-of-the-dice-the-rising.md" >}})" during his Broadway show at the Walter Kerr Theater in New York City–the two songs switch places for the third time.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/10/2017-1003.jpg.webp)

**2022:** Bruce releases "Nightshift," the second single from his upcoming album, *Only the Strong Survive.*<br>
