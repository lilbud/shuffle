***New for 2025:** No updates*<br>

**1976:** At the Fox Theater in Atlanta, Bruce premieres an early version of "[Frankie]({{< relref "2019-03-31-roll-of-the-dice-frankie.md" >}})."<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/03/1976-03-26.png.webp)

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/03/1976-0326.jpg.webp)

**1988:** "[Ain't Got You]({{< relref "1987-01-01-roll-of-the-dice-aint-got-you.md" >}})" makes its live debut during Bruce's Tunnel of Love Express show at the Rupp Arena in Lexington.<br>

**1992:** *CBS This Morning* previews Bruce's upcoming albums, *Human Touch* and *Lucky Town*.<br>

{{< youtube DkC5FrphrVg >}}

**2003:** Bruce and the E Street Band play their second of two nights in Brisbane on the Rising Tour, opening with an acoustic "[Born in the U.S.A.]({{< relref "1984-01-03-roll-of-the-dice-born-in-the-u-s-a.md" >}})" and featuring a rare "[I'm a Rocker]({{< relref "2020-09-18-roll-of-the-dice-im-a-rocker.md" >}})."<br>

{{< videopress id=yyUIuX3l >}}

**2012:** Peter Wolf guest-stars for "[Raise Your Hand]({{< relref "1976-01-13-cover-me-raise-your-hand.md" >}}/)" (a tour premiere) in the encores at this Wrecking Ball show at Boston's TD Garden. The show also features tour premieres of "[Thundercrack]({{< relref "2021-09-24-roll-of-the-dice-thundercrack.md" >}})," "[Jackson Cage,]({{< relref "2018-11-04-roll-of-the-dice-jackson-cage.md" >}})" and "[She's the One]({{< relref "2021-04-04-roll-of-the-dice-shes-the-one.md" >}})."<br>
