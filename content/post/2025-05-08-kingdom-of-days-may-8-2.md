***New for 2025:** New entry for 1975; video for 2000; audio for 2000 and 2003*<br>

**1972:** John Hammond sends the demos that Bruce recorded on May 3 to Clive Davis, president of Columbia Records. He includes a note that reads:<br>

> *Here is a copy of a couple of the reels of Bruce Springsteen, a very talented kid who recorded these twelve songs in a period of around two hours last Wednesday . . . I think we better act quickly because many people heard the boy at The Gaslight so that his fame is beginning to spread.*

**1975:** Bruce records an early take of “[Linda Let Me Be the One]({{< relref "2021-03-27-roll-of-the-dice-linda-let-me-be-the-one.md" >}})” at the Record Plant in New York City.<br>

**1976:** Bruce continues his Chicken Scratch Tour of the south with a show at Louisiana State University in Baton Rouge.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/05/1976-0508.jpg.webp)

**1981:** Night Two in Stockholm for the original River Tour opens with Bruce’s last known performance of “[Run Through the Jungle]({{< relref "2018-04-05-cover-me-run-through-the-jungle.md" >}})” and features the European debut of “[The Price You Pay]({{< relref "2019-09-23-roll-of-the-dice-the-price-you-pay.md" >}}).”<br>

At the party, Bruce receives his official award for *[The Ghost of Tom Joad]({{< relref "2023-06-03-album-companion-the-ghost-of-tom-joad.md" >}})*reaching platinum sales status in Europe. **2003:** The second of two Rising shows at Stadion Feyenoord in Rotterdam features a solo acoustic “[Darkness on the Edge of Town]({{< relref "2016-01-07-roll-of-the-dice-darkness-on-the-edge-of-town.md" >}}).”<br>

**2005:** *Devils & Dust* captures the Number One slot on the U.S. album chart, Bruce’s 13th studio album to accomplish that feat.<br>

**2006:** As the Seeger Sessions Tour rolls into London, Bruce plays his first show at the Hammersmith Odeon (now called the Hammersmith Apollo) since his original famed 1975 stand.<br>

**2009:** Bruce takes the Working on a Dream Tour to Penn State University, where he performs “[My Generation]({{< relref "2018-03-27-matr-pete-townshend-roger-daltry-and-bruce-springsteen-my-generation.md" >}})” for the first time. The set also includes the tour premieres of “This Life,” “[Bobby Jean]({{< relref "1984-01-10-roll-of-the-dice-bobby-jean.md" >}}),” and “[Wooly Bully]({{< relref "2018-03-31-cover-me-woolly-bully.md" >}}).”<br>
