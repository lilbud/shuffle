The Cowboy Junkies are no strangers to covering Bruce's music in their inimitably sad style. Their stark covers of "[State Trooper]({{< relref "2019-12-22-roll-of-the-dice-state-trooper.md" >}})" and "Thunder Road" are right up there among the best Springsteen covers out there.

But this one's different.

Margo Timmins' solo turn on "[If I Should Fall Behind]({{< relref "1992-02-05-roll-of-the-dice-if-i-should-fall-behind.md" >}})" is delicate, sweet, and so intimate you can hear the piano pedals.

{{< youtube ugCpitUSvDI >}}

Featuring Margo on lead vocals, Nick Craine on backgrounds (himself no stranger to Bruce's music), and Witold Grabowiecki on piano, and Jeff Bird on bass, this darn-near perfect cover stems from Margo's 2009 under-the-radar *Margo's Corner* recordings.

It's one of my favorite performances of one of Bruce's best songs.
