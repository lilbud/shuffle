***New for 2025:** Expanded entry and audio for 1979; video for 2023; new entry for 2024*<br>

**1976:** The Chicken Scratch Tour kicks off with a show at the Township Auditorium in Columbia, South Carolina–Bruce's first time performing in the state.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2024/03/The_Columbia_Record_1976_03_26_page_6.jpg.webp)

**1977:** The Lawsuit Tour comes to a close with a final show at Boston's Music Hall, featuring the last "[Don't Look Back]({{< relref "2020-05-03-roll-of-the-dice-dont-look-back.md" >}})" for 22 years, the last "[(Your Love Keeps Lifting Me) Higher and Higher]({{< relref "2018-09-23-cover-me-your-love-keeps-lifting-me-higher-and-higher.md" >}})" for 32 years, and the last "[Action in the Streets]({{< relref "2022-06-27-roll-of-the-dice-action-in-the-streets.md" >}})" for 34 years.<br>

**1979:** At his home studio in Holmdel, Bruce and the E Street Band rehearse "[Meet Me in the City]({{< relref "2020-08-23-roll-of-the-dice-meet-me-in-the-city.md" >}})."<br>

{{< youtube -KuFJEzoFuE >}}

That evening, Bruce catches The Ramones playing at The Fast Lane in Asbury Park. Backstage, Joey Ramone asks Bruce to write a song for them. Bruce starts writing "[Hungry Heart]({{< relref "2022-12-21-roll-of-the-dice-hungry-heart.md" >}})" that very night.

**1984:** Bruce joins Cats on a Smooth Surface on-stage at The Stone Pony in Asbury Park for "[I'm Bad, I'm Nationwide]({{< relref "2020-02-17-cover-me-im-bad-im-nationwide.md" >}})" and "[Lucille]({{< relref "1976-01-07-cover-me-lucille.md" >}}/)."<br>

**1996:** Bruce performs "[Dead Man Walkin']({{< relref "2018-07-24-roll-of-the-dice-dead-man-walkin.md" >}})" at the Academy Awards, where it is nominated (but loses) for Best Original Song. Watch the CNN news coverage and full performance:<br>
