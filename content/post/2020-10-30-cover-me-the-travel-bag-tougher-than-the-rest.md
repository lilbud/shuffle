I had a completely different cover queued up for today and a week’s worth of posts written to follow.

But yesterday morning, I came across a brand new cover of “[Tougher Than the Rest]({{< relref "1987-01-02-roll-of-the-dice-tougher-than-the-rest.md" >}}/)” by an Italian duo called The Travel Bag, and I’ve played it about a dozen times since.

(I know next to nothing about The Travel Bag except that they consist of Jole Maddalena on vocals and Claudio D’amico on guitar, they’ve got an original [album](https://open.spotify.com/artist/2g2DcaxnQiHrezeaNOzm3n) you can stream on the major streaming services, their [YouTube page](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCZvqn0hu-6_KK5JUB2LGb0w) is full of beautiful cover performances, and you should definitely check them out.)

So since I can’t seem to shake my obsession with this absolutely gorgeous-in-every-way performance, I’m bumping the queue so you can enjoy it along with me.

Have a great weekend.
