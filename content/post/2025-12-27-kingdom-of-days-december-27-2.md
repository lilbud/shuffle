***New for 2025:** New entry for 1977*<br>

**1967:** The Castiles play a show at Le Teendezvous in New Shrewsbury, New Jersey.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/12/1967-1227.jpg.webp)

**1968:** Earth plays four sets at Le Teendezvous in New Shrewsbury.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/12/1968-1227.jpg.webp)

**1973:** Bruce and the E Street Band kick off a four-night/two-shows-nightly stand at The Main Point in Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/12/1973-1227.jpg.webp)

**1975:** Bruce and the band play their first of a four-night year-closing stand at the Tower Theater in Philadelphia. Tonight sees the tour premiere of "[Back in the U.S.A.]({{< relref "1976-01-03-cover-me-back-in-the-u-s-a.md" >}}/)" and the last full-band "[Lost in the Flood]({{< relref "2011-01-05-roll-of-the-dice-lost-in-the-flood.md" >}}/)" for 25 years.<br>
