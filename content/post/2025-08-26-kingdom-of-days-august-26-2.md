***New for 2025:** New entry for 1977 and 1979*<br>

**1970:** Steel Mill rehearses in Wanamassa, New Jersey for their upcoming appearance at the Nashville Music Festival.<br>

**1973:** Bruce and the E Street Band wrap a week-long residency at Oliver's in Boston.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/08/1973-0826.jpg.webp)

**1977:** Bruce and the band work on "[Talk to Me]({{< relref "2019-02-26-roll-of-the-dice-talk-to-me.md" >}})" during a recording session in New York City.<br>

**1978:** The Darkness Tour descends on Rhode Island, for a show at the Providence Civic Center.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2019/08/1978-08-26.jpg.webp)

**1979:** Bruce and the E Street Band record "[The River]({{< relref "2021-03-04-roll-of-the-dice-the-river.md" >}})" at the Power Station in New York City.<br>

{{< youtube BjSsFvrrh7g >}}

**1984:** Bruce and the E Street Band play their second of two nights at Washington DC's Capital Center in Largo, where they play "[Be True]({{< relref "2020-12-02-roll-of-the-dice-be-true.md" >}})" for the very first time.<br>

{{< youtube E5Dis2XlJ4A >}}

**1985:** The Born in the U.S.A. stadium tour moves north to CNE Stadium in Toronto for the first of two shows.<br>

{{< youtube OfuZPBFYwBY >}}

{{< youtube P9Zzw3qz7Dk >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/08/1985-08-26a.png.webp)

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/08/1985-08-26b.png.webp)

**1987:** Bruce joins Jah Love on stage at Key Largo in Belmar, New Jersey, for reggae versions of "[My Hometown]({{< relref "1983-01-12-roll-of-the-dice-my-hometown.md" >}})," "[Jersey Girl]({{< relref "1976-02-07-cover-me-roll-of-the-dice-jersey-girl.md" >}}/)," and "[Born in the U.S.A.]({{< relref "1984-01-03-roll-of-the-dice-born-in-the-u-s-a.md" >}})" Afterwards, he zips over to The Columns in Avon, where he performs "[Lucille]({{< relref "1976-01-07-cover-me-lucille.md" >}}/)" and "[Stand By Me]({{< relref "2021-12-12-matr-u2-and-bruce-springsteen-stand-by-me.md" >}})" with The Cherubs.<br>

The Trews, Tom Cochrane, and Peter Elkas make a guest appearance for the show-closing "Twist and Shout."<br>

**2021:** *Springsteen on Broadway* continues its limited return engagement at the St. James Theater in New York City.<br>

**2023:** Bruce and the E Street Band play their second of two shows at Gillette Stadium in Foxborough, where "[Lonesome Day]({{< relref "2020-04-11-roll-of-the-dice-lonesome-day.md" >}})" and "[Seven Nights to Rock]({{< relref "2021-04-22-cover-me-seven-nights-to-rock.md" >}})" make their tour debuts.<br>
