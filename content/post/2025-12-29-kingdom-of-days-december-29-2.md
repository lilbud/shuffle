***New for 2025:** New entry for 1977*<br>

**1972:** ~~Rick~~ Bruce plays in Ohio for the first time, when he and the band open for Sha Na Na in Dayton.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2019/12/1972-12-29.jpg.webp)

**1973:** Bruce and his unnamed band play two shows on their third of four nights at The Main Point in Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania.<br>

**1974:** Bruce makes a guest appearance with Southside Johnny and Steve Van Zandt's Blackberry Booze Band at The Stone Pony in Asbury Park.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2019/12/1974-12-29.jpg.webp)

**1975:** On his night off during his three-night stand in Philadelphia, Bruce takes in the J. Geils Band show at the Spectrum from the press box (along with actress Faye Dunaway).<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/12/1975-12-29.png.webp)

**1977:** Bruce and the E Street Band record for *Darkness on the Edge of Town* at The Record Plant in New York City.<br>

**1980:** At Night Two of three in Uniondale on the River Tour, Bruce opens with the tour premiere of "[Night]({{< relref "2019-10-31-roll-of-the-dice-night.md" >}})." Toward the end of the show comes another premiere: a tour one-off of "[Incident on 57th Street]({{< relref "2018-12-14-roll-of-the-dice-incident-on-57th-street.md" >}})." That performance of "Incident" will be released as a B-side years later, and "[Darkness on the Edge of Town,]({{< relref "2016-01-07-roll-of-the-dice-darkness-on-the-edge-of-town.md" >}})" "This Land is Your Land," and "[You Can Look (But You Better Not Touch)]({{< relref "2020-06-14-roll-of-the-dice-you-can-look-but-you-better-not-touch.md" >}})" will be officially released on *Live 1975-85.* This show is available as part of Bruce's official archive series–get it [here](https://live.brucespringsteen.net/live-music/0,22391/Bruce-Springsteen---The-E-Street-Band-mp3-flac-download-12-29-1980-Nassau-Veterans-Memorial-Coliseum-Uniondale-NY.html).<br>
