***New for 2025:** New entry for 1982; audio for 2008; video for 2023*<br>

**1973:** Bruce opens for Jerry Lee Lewis and Chuck Berry at the University of Maryland. After his set, Bruce and the E Street Band (plus Southside Johnny) serve as the backing band at no charge for Lewis and Berry, remaining on stage the entire night. The band has no rehearsal time with Berry, who keeps the band on their toes the entire night. Watch Bruce tell the story below. (Thanks to Dave Homorody for the photo!)<br>

**2008:** Greensboro gets a fantastic Magic show, as Bruce opens with the tour premieres of "[Roulette]({{< relref "2022-04-16-roll-of-the-dice-roulette.md" >}})" and "[Don't Look Back]({{< relref "2020-05-03-roll-of-the-dice-dont-look-back.md" >}})," before segueing to "[Radio Nowhere]({{< relref "2021-08-01-roll-of-the-dice-radio-nowhere.md" >}})," "[Out in the Street]({{< relref "2021-10-28-roll-of-the-dice-out-in-the-street.md" >}})," and "[The Promised Land]({{< relref "2016-01-09-roll-of-the-dice-the-promised-land.md" >}})." Opening packs don't get much better than that. Also tour premiering tonight: "[Mary's Place]({{< relref "2022-03-25-roll-of-the-dice-marys-place.md" >}})." This show is available as part of Bruce's official archive series–get it [here](https://live.brucespringsteen.net/live-music/0,25606/Bruce-Springsteen---The-E-Street-Band-mp3-flac-download-4-28-2008-Greensboro-Coliseum-Greensboro-NC.html).<br>

**2009:** Philadelphia always seems to get great shows, and the Working on a Dream Tour keeps up the tradition. For more highlights from this show, see my [Where the Band Was report]({{< relref "2018-04-28-where-the-band-was-philadelphia-april-28-2009.md" >}}) from this date.<br>
