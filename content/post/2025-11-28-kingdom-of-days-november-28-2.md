***New for 2025:** New entry for 1977*<br>

**1971:** The Bruce Springsteen Band is in residence at the Student Prince in Asbury Park.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/11/1971-11-27.png.webp)

**1977:** Bruce holds a recording session at The Record Plant in New York City, where he and the band work on "[Racing in the Street]({{< relref "2021-10-03-roll-of-the-dice-racing-in-the-street.md" >}})."<br>

**1978:** Bruce and the E Street Band bring the Darkness Tour to the Dane County Memorial Coliseum in Madison, Wisconsin.<br>

{{< youtube 18_SB3a9Jbo >}}

**![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2019/11/47544237051_e03acb5d43_o.jpg.webp)**<br>

**1980:** On Black Friday, Bruce and the band play their second of two nights at Madison Square Garden in New York City. This show features a very unusual "[Ramrod]({{< relref "2022-09-10-roll-of-the-dice-ramrod.md" >}})"/"[Living on the Edge of the World]({{< relref "2018-10-18-roll-of-the-dice-living-on-the-edge-of-the-world.md" >}})" (the latter of which hadn't even been released) mashup, as well as the tour premiere of "[4th of July, Asbury Park (Sandy)]({{< relref "2019-04-26-roll-of-the-dice-4th-of-july-asbury-park-sandy.md" >}})."<br>

{{< youtube 4HUThNRDG0E >}}

{{< youtube-playlist PLJTyL9twk3zA4JUhmcl5oNlOk1Mn6t5dg >}}

**1999:** The first leg of the Reunion Tour enters its last stand when Bruce and the E Street Band play their first of two nights at the Target Center in Minneapolis. The holidays must be approaching–Bruce closes the show with the tour premiere of "[Santa Claus is Comin' to Town]({{< relref "2021-12-24-cover-me-santa-claus-is-comin-to-town.md" >}})."<br>

{{< youtube WGTS8ii7nrU >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/11/1999-11-28.png.webp)

**2008:** Bruce releases "[My Lucky Day,]({{< relref "2019-09-02-roll-of-the-dice-my-lucky-day.md" >}})" the second single from his upcoming album, *Working on a Dream*.<br>

{{< youtube gO7vFDfhDHw >}}

**2012:** Bruce continues shaking up the Wrecking Ball set list at the Rose Garden in Portland, opening with "[Land of Hope and Dreams]({{< relref "2018-01-05-roll-of-the-dice-land-of-hope-and-dreams.md" >}})," and featuring rare outings for "[Loose Ends]({{< relref "2022-06-23-roll-of-the-dice-loose-end.md" >}})," "[Seeds]({{< relref "2020-10-10-roll-of-the-dice-seeds.md" >}})," and "[Drive All Night]({{< relref "2018-02-27-roll-of-the-dice-drive-all-night.md" >}})." For more highlights from this show, see my [*Where the Band Was* report.]({{< relref "2018-11-28-where-the-band-was-portland-november-28-2012.md" >}})<br>
