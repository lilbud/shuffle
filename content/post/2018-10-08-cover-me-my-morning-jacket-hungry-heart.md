Days before their appearance at a Hurricane Sandy relief concert in Asbury Park, My Morning Jacket premiered their note-perfect cover of “[Hungry Heart]({{< relref "2022-12-21-roll-of-the-dice-hungry-heart.md" >}})” at their New Year’s Eve show in Boston. (They played it in AP, too.)

It’s a wonderfully faithful cover without a trace of irony or self-consciousness. There’s even a horn section.

I’m pretty certain Bruce would approve.
