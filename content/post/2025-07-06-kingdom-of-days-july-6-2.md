***New for 2025:** New entry for 1975; audio for 1985*<br>

**1968:** The Castiles play a gig at Le Teendezvous in New Shrewsbury, New Jersey.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/07/1968-0706.jpg.webp)

**1973:** Bruce and the E Street Band headline two shows at The Main Point in Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2019/06/mainpoint.jpg.webp)

**1975:** Bruce and the E Street Band work on "[Backstreets]({{< relref "2022-05-14-roll-of-the-dice-backstreets.md" >}})" at a *Born to Run* recording session at the Record Plant in New York City.<br>

**1978:** Bruce joins Gary Busey and The Old Dog Band on stage for "[Carol]({{< relref "2022-02-17-cover-me-carol.md" >}})" at the Sundance Saloon in Calabasas, California.<br>

**1981:** Bruce and the boys play their fourth of six nights at the Brendan Byrne Arena's opening stand. Recordings of "[Independence Day]({{< relref "2022-03-14-roll-of-the-dice-independence-day.md" >}})," "[Cadillac Ranch]({{< relref "2020-07-23-roll-of-the-dice-cadillac-ranch.md" >}})," and "[Racing in the Street]({{< relref "2021-10-03-roll-of-the-dice-racing-in-the-street.md" >}})" will eventually be released on *Live 1975-85*.<br>

**1984:** Night Two in Cincinnati on the Born in the U.S.A. Tour. Listen in on the soundcheck below, including a never-played "The Good, The Bad, and The Ugly."<br>

{{< videopress id=mB3khd4m >}}

**1985:** Bruce and the E Street Band close out their Wembley Stadium stand with their third of three nights in London. Steve Van Zandt makes his third consecutive encore appearance. Also in attendance tonight: Phil Collins, Mark Knopfler, Roger Taylor, and John Entwhistle.<br>

**1989:** *Nebraska* is certified platinum and [*Darkness on the Edge of Town*]({{< relref "2022-07-05-album-companion-darkness-on-the-edge-of-town.md" >}}) is certified double platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America.<br>

**1992:** Bruce and his new touring band kick off a five-night stand at Wembley Arena in London, the last stand of the European leg of World Tour 1992. The setlist features a rare "[With Every Wish.]({{< relref "2018-01-18-roll-of-the-dice-with-every-wish.md" >}})"<br>
