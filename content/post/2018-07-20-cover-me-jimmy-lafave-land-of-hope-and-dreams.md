Oklahoma singer-songwriter Jimmy LaFave has a talent for interpreting Bruce's songs (see his cover of "[Valentine's Day]({{< relref "2018-03-03-cover-me-jimmy-lafave-valentines-day.md" >}})").

In 2012, Jimmy released his album *Depending on the Distance*, featuring his cover of Bruce's "[Land of Hope and Dreams.]({{< relref "2018-01-05-roll-of-the-dice-land-of-hope-and-dreams.md" >}})" Unlike Bruce's version, which plays more like a declarative anthem, Jimmy's version is tender–almost a love song. It's a different take, and a great cover.
