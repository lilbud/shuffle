Sometimes a wonderful cover can fly completely under the radar.

This is one of them.

{{< youtube Ufyu-DgD5tA >}}

Lanny Cordola has associated himself with a few bands over the years, but he’s a wonderful musician in his own right. (He plays every instrument you hear on this track.)

His 2001 spin on “[My Beautiful Reward]({{< relref "1992-02-11-roll-of-the-dice-my-beautiful-reward.md" >}})” is original in sound but true in spirit to the original; it’s more reflective and atmospheric than Bruce’s album version, and his vocals and arrangement really bring out the narrative.

This is very well done.
