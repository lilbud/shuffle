***New for 2025:** Video for 2022*<br>

**1973:** Bruce and the E Street Band headline the first two shows of a three-night/six-show stand at My Father's Place in Roslyn, New York. WLIR-FM broadcasts the late show live.<br>

**1978:** The Darkness Tour continues its southern swing, arriving in Columbia, South Carolina, for a show at the Township Auditorium.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2024/07/The_Columbia_Record_1978_08_01_30-scaled.jpg.webp)

**1982:** Bruce makes a guest appearance with Sonny Kenn and the Wild Ideas at Big Man's West in Red Bank.<br>

{{< videopress id=lzcXNflW >}}

**1984:** "[Cover Me]({{< relref "1984-01-04-roll-of-the-dice-cover-me.md" >}})" is released as the second single off of the red-hot *Born in the U.S.A.* album. It will peak at #7 on the charts.<br>

{{< youtube s3tqIk8TPWM >}}

That evening, Bruce tour premieres "[Growin' Up]({{< relref "2011-01-03-roll-of-the-dice-growin-up.md" >}}/)" at Night Two in Detroit on the Born in the U.S.A. Tour.

{{< videopress id=1IZ822FI >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/07/1984-07-31.png.webp)

**1987:** Bruce guest-stars with Marshall Crenshaw at The Stone Pony in Asbury Park for "[You Can't Sit Down](../2018-06-21-cover-me-you-cant-sit-down/post.md/)," "La Bamba," and "Twist and Shout."<br>

{{< videopress id=91vNP1rr >}}

**1992:** The opening stand of World Tour 1992 reaches its midpoint in East Rutherford on Night Six of eleven. Many tour premieres tonight, including "[Atlantic City]({{< relref "2018-06-04-roll-of-the-dice-atlantic-city.md" >}})," "[Spirit in the Night]({{< relref "2011-01-08-roll-of-the-dice-spirit-in-the-night.md" >}}/)," "[I'm on Fire]({{< relref "1984-01-08-roll-of-the-dice-im-on-fire.md" >}})", and the live debuts of "[Pony Boy]({{< relref "2019-03-01-roll-of-the-dice-cover-me-pony-boy.md" >}})" and "[Cross My Heart]({{< relref "2020-12-29-roll-of-the-dice-cross-my-heart.md" >}})."<br>

{{< youtube xeSRlWZq2lk >}}

{{< youtube zESafYXuPiM >}}

**2001:** Bruce makes a surprise guest appearance at the Count Basie Theater in Red Bank, joining Bruce Hornsby for the final encore of "[Cadillac Ranch]({{< relref "2020-07-23-roll-of-the-dice-cadillac-ranch.md" >}})."<br>

{{< videopress id=EQ77Afpd >}}

**2005:** Bruce plays a stunning acoustic show in Columbus, playing "[Lift Me Up]({{< relref "2018-05-20-roll-of-the-dice-lift-me-up.md" >}})" for the first time ever, and tour premiering "[Cynthia]({{< relref "2019-09-06-roll-of-the-dice-cynthia.md" >}})" and "[Back in Your Arms.]({{< relref "2019-11-10-roll-of-the-dice-back-in-your-arms.md" >}})" This show is available as part of Bruce's official archive series (get it [here](https://live.brucespringsteen.net/live-music/0,13061/Bruce-Springsteen-mp3-flac-download-7-31-2005-Schottenstein-Center-Columbus-OH.html)), and it's a wonderful listen.<br>

**2008:** At the final of three Giants Stadium shows on the Magic Tour, Bruce tour premieres "[Pretty Flamingo]({{< relref "2021-12-04-cover-me-pretty-flamingo.md" >}})" and "[Jersey Girl]({{< relref "1976-02-07-cover-me-roll-of-the-dice-jersey-girl.md" >}}/)" and holds a mini-Seeger Sessions reunion when Jeremy Chatzky, Sam Bardfeld, and Curtis King join the band for "[American Land.]({{< relref "2016-01-22-roll-of-the-dice-american-land.md" >}})"<br>

{{< youtube y1Pht9wCmdc >}}

**2012:** Bruce wraps up the first European leg of the Wrecking Ball Tour with a marathon of a show in Helsinki–his longest ever, over four hours–and that's not even including a five-song pre-show! Five songs have their tour premieres, including "[Back in Your Arms]({{< relref "2019-11-10-roll-of-the-dice-back-in-your-arms.md" >}})", "[Detroit Medley,]({{< relref "1976-01-02-cover-me-detroit-medley.md" >}}/)" and covers of "[I Don't Want to Go Home]({{< relref "2018-03-30-meeting-across-the-river-bruce-springsteen-and-steve-van-zandt-i-dont-want-to-go-home.md" >}})," "Rockin' All Over the World," and "[(Your Love Keeps Lifting Me) Higher and Higher]({{< relref "2018-09-23-cover-me-your-love-keeps-lifting-me-higher-and-higher.md" >}})." Plus: rare outings for "[Loose Ends]({{< relref "2022-06-23-roll-of-the-dice-loose-end.md" >}})," "[Be True]({{< relref "2020-12-02-roll-of-the-dice-be-true.md" >}})," "[Light of Day]({{< relref "2018-12-06-roll-of-the-dice-light-of-day.md" >}})," and more–an absolutely amazing show. No surprise that it's part of Bruce's official archive series–get it [here](https://live.brucespringsteen.net/live-music/0,16397/Bruce-Springsteen---The-E-Street-Band-mp3-flac-download-7-31-2012-Olympiastadion-Helsinki-FI.html).<br>
