From the 1995 opening concert for the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame: Chuck Berry backed by Bruce Springsteen and the E Street Band.

{{< youtube 6swgiM9vSEE >}}

“Johnny B. Goode,” of course, is one of the most recognizable songs in rock and roll. Chuck Berry’s original version from 1958 peaked at #8 on the Billboard Hot 100, but it went on to be covered by scores of artists and ranks seventh in Rolling Stone’s 500 Greatest Songs of All Time.

{{< youtube Uf4rxCB4lys >}}

**Johnny B. Goode**<br>
**First performed:** April 28, 1973 (College Park, MD)<br>
**Last performed:** April 21, 2017 (Asbury Park, NJ)<br>
