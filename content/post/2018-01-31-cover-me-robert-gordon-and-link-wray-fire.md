Robert Gordon and Link Wray were the first to record and release Bruce Springsteen’s “[Fire]({{< relref "2020-08-05-roll-of-the-dice-fire.md" >}}).” Bruce gave Gordon the song after seeing him perform with Wray; they released it in 1978, years before Bruce’s version debuted on *Live 1975-1985*, and decades before the Bruce’s studio version surfaced on *The Promise.*

This recording from a BBC studio session very well be the definitive take on the song–just listen to Link Wray go to town on it.
