C’mon, admit it: who among us hasn’t wondered what it would sound like if The Ramones and The Go-Gos teamed up to cover “[Radio Nowhere]({{< relref "2021-08-01-roll-of-the-dice-radio-nowhere.md" >}})?”

Well, wonder no longer. because this delightful cover by Liverpool band Town Bike is pretty much exactly that. And I love it so much!
