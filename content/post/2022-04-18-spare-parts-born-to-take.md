No matter how many times I hear this, I can't help cracking up at.... well, you'll know it when you hear it.

[Watch Video](https://bandcamp.com/EmbeddedPlayer/album=3964793550/size=large/bgcol=ffffff/linkcol=0687f5/tracklist=false/artwork=small/track=887175261/transparent=true/)

I don't know who RuinedMusic2000 is, but I do know they've got some serious and seamless mash-up talent and a clever sense of humor. Who would have thought "Born to Run" would layer so perfectly on top of A-ha's "Take On Me?"

But wait, there's more: As great a song as it is, it's the video that makes "Take on Me" a classic. RuinedMusic2000 took a crack at mashing that up too, and the result does not disappoint.
