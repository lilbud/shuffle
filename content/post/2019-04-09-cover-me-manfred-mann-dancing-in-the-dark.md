Everyone knows Manfred Mann’s cover of Bruce’s “[Blinded by the Light]({{< relref "2011-01-02-roll-of-the-dice-blinded-by-the-light.md" >}}).” True Springsteen fans know Manfred Mann’s Earth Band [also covered]({{< relref "2018-06-14-cover-me-manfred-manns-earth-band-for-you.md" >}}) “[For You]({{< relref "2011-01-07-roll-of-the-dice-for-you.md" >}})” and “[Spirit in the Night]({{< relref "2011-01-08-roll-of-the-dice-spirit-in-the-night.md" >}}/).”

But the band’s most obscure Springsteen adaptation is actually a cover of Bruce’s greatest hit.

From 2009, check out this brilliant mash-up of “[Dancing in the Dark]({{< relref "1984-01-13-roll-of-the-dice-dancing-in-the-dark.md" >}})” with “House of the Rising Sun” by The Animals.
