***New for 2025:** New entry for 1981; video for 2002 and 2004*<br>

**1975:** Bruce and the E Street Band usher in the holiday season with their first of two shows at Boston's Music Hall. On the set list: Bruce's very first performance of "[Party Lights]({{< relref "2022-06-01-cover-me-party-lights.md" >}})," and the earliest captured performance of "[Santa Claus is Comin' to Town]({{< relref "2021-12-24-cover-me-santa-claus-is-comin-to-town.md" >}})."<br>

**2004:** Bruce joins Joe Grushecky and The Houserockers for a special Flood Aid concert in Pittsburgh, benefiting local residents impacted by Hurricane Ivan. Bruce joins opening act Exit 105 for "[All Along the Watchtower]({{< relref "2020-08-13-matr-bruce-springsteen-neil-young-and-exit-105-all-along-the-watchtower.md" >}})" before playing a brief acoustic set and finally joining Joe and the Band for their set. Jesse Malin makes a guest appearance on the show-closing "Santa Claus is Comin' to Town."<br>
