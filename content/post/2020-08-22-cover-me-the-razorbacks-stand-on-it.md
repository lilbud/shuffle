"[Stand On It]({{< relref "2021-10-08-roll-of-the-dice-stand-on-it.md" >}})" is not an easy song to cover–it's as rockabilly as rockabilly gets, and rockabilly is a lost art form these days. But there are a few bands out there keeping rockabilly alive, and one of them hails from Pottsville, just an hour's drive from Asbury Park.

The Razorbacks released their live album, *Home of the Razorbacks*, way back in 2001, and they opened both their set and the CD with their deservedly confident cover of "Stand On It."

{{< youtube zNR78ji2tFk >}}

If you like what you heard, check out the rest of the album [here](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=OLAK5uy_n1vSZCVuEM-E85YSOp6FUP--DRycyMRik).

The Razorbacks are still touring (or at least they were, pre-COVID), so keep an eye out when the dust clears–you might be able to catch this one live.
