***New for 2025:** Reviews for 1973; new entry for 1977 and 1983*<br>

**1973:** Bruce and his band open for Chicago at the Civic Center in Springfield.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2025/06/The_Republican_1973_06_11_11-scaled.jpg.webp)

**1977:** Bruce and the E Street Band hold recording sessions for what will become *Darkness on the Edge of Town* at Atlantic Studios in New York City.<br>

**1978:** When the Darkness Tour stops in Bloomington, Minnesota, snippets of several songs are filmed on Super-8 film by fans.<br>

**1983:** Bruce and the E Street Band record "[Richfield Whistle]({{< relref "1983-01-15-roll-of-the-dice-richfield-whistle.md" >}})" at The Hit Factory in New York City. As of this writing, it has yet to see an official release (but stay tuned).<br>

**1984:** Bruce and Nils join Cats on a Smooth Surface at the band's Stone Pony gig.<br>

**1988:** Bruce and the E Street Band play a warm-up set at Stadio Comunale in Turin, the day before the kick-off of the European leg of the Tunnel of Love Tour.<br>

**2003:** "[Tenth Avenue Freeze-Out]({{< relref "2018-11-19-roll-of-the-dice-tenth-avenue-freeze-out.md" >}})" makes its first post-Reunion appearance at Bruce's Rising show in Munich. Bruce opens the show with a solo acoustic "[Born in the U.S.A.]({{< relref "1984-01-03-roll-of-the-dice-born-in-the-u-s-a.md" >}})" and plays "[Jackson Cage]({{< relref "2018-11-04-roll-of-the-dice-jackson-cage.md" >}})" in Germany for the first time.<br>

**2012:** The Italian leg of the Wrecking Ball Tour continues with a show at Stadio Artemio Franchi in Florence, where fans witness the tour premieres of "[Be True]({{< relref "2020-12-02-roll-of-the-dice-be-true.md" >}})" and "[Burning Love]({{< relref "2018-05-01-cover-me-burning-love.md" >}})."<br>

**2013:** Bruce arrives in London for his Wrecking Ball show and sings "[The Promised Land]({{< relref "2016-01-09-roll-of-the-dice-the-promised-land.md" >}})" (in support of ONE) with an acoustic guitar from the balcony of MTV Networks Europe. Watch the performance below–the video is wonderfully shot and recorded.<br>

{{< youtube NtAlPiVtqD0 >}}

**2018:** Bruce receives a special Tony Award from Billy Joel for *Springsteen on Broadway*. He performs "[My Hometown]({{< relref "1983-01-12-roll-of-the-dice-my-hometown.md" >}})" at the televised ceremonies (introduced by Robert De Niro), along with an abridged version of its introduction from the show.<br>
