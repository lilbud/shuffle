**1978:** At Night Two at Atlanta's Fox Theater on the Darkness Tour, Bruce opens with his live debut of "[The Last Time]({{< relref "2020-07-24-cover-me-the-last-time.md" >}})" by The Rolling Stones, plays "[Kitty's Back]({{< relref "2022-01-22-roll-of-the-dice-kittys-back.md" >}})" for the last time for 22 years, and performs "[It's My Life]({{< relref "2021-11-23-cover-me-its-my-life.md" >}})" for the last time to date. **2004:** The Vote for Change Tour kicks off at the Wachovia Center in Philadelphia, with Bright Eyes and R.E.M. opening for Bruce and the E Street Band, aiming to generate voter enthusiasm and registration for the upcoming U.S. presidential election. Bruce appears with R.E.M. during their set to play "[Man on the Moon]({{< relref "2021-01-06-matr-r-e-m-and-bruce-springsteen-man-on-the-moon.md" >}})," and the band returns the favor during his set (Michael Stipe joins on "Because the Night" (with Patti Smith's lyrics), and both Michael and Peter join for "Born to Run"). John Fogerty plays a mini-set within Bruce's set, a nightly highlight of the brief tour. Bruce opens his set with a gorgeous acoustic "[Star-Spangled Banner,]({{< relref "2021-01-20-cover-me-the-star-spangled-banner.md" >}})" and all artists join for a star-studded encore.<br>

**2006:** Bruce and the Sessions Band kick off the final leg of the Seeger Sessions Tour with an autumn tour of Italy, starting in Bologna with a show at PalaMalaguti, where "[The River]({{< relref "2021-03-04-roll-of-the-dice-the-river.md" >}})" makes its tour debut in a beautiful new arrangement, and Bruce plays "[This Little Light of Mine]({{< relref "1976-01-31-cover-me-this-little-light-of-mine.md" >}}/)" for the very first time. "[American Land]({{< relref "2016-01-22-roll-of-the-dice-american-land.md" >}})" returns as the show closer, where it will remain for the rest of the tour.<br>
