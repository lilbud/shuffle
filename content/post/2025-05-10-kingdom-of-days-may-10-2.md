***New for 2025:** New entry for 1978 and 1982; review for 2005; video for 2006*<br>

**1970:** Steel Mill headlines an outdoor concert at Monmouth College in West Long Branch, New Jersey.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/05/1970-0510.jpg.webp)

**1974:** A scheduled gig in Providence, Rhode Island is cancelled to make room for recording sessions for *Born to Run*.<br>

**1975:** Bruce records "[Night]({{< relref "2019-10-31-roll-of-the-dice-night.md" >}})" at the Record Plant in New York City.<br>

{{< youtube MMuFg-ntDKI >}}

**1976:** Night Two at the Municipal Auditorium Theater in Mobile, Alabama on the Chicken Scratch Tour.<br>

{{< videopress id=1U5KUucD >}}

**1978:** Less than a month before the release of *Darkness on the Edge of Town*, Bruce is at the Record Plant in New York city, working on mixing and overdubs.<br>

**1982:** Bruce records "[Wages of Sin]({{< relref "2020-02-07-roll-of-the-dice-wages-of-sin.md" >}})" at the Power Station in New York City. It won't see release for another sixteen years.<br>

{{< youtube U5i69qRqL_0 >}}

**1988:** Bruce plays "[Cautious Man]({{< relref "1987-01-05-roll-of-the-dice-cautious-man.md" >}}/)" for the very first time (and the very last for 17 years) at Night Two in Bloomington. This show also features the premiere of "[Boom Boom]({{< relref "2020-03-26-cover-me-boom-boom.md" >}})."<br>

**1997:** Night Two for Bruce in Warsaw during his first visit to Poland features the final performance to date of "[There Will Never Be Any Other for Me But You]({{< relref "2019-08-23-roll-of-the-dice-there-will-never-be-any-other-for-me-but-you.md" >}})."<br>

**2005:** At his solo acoustic show at the Xcel Energy Center in St. Paul, Bruce performs "[A Good Man is Hard to Find (Pittsburgh)]({{< relref "2021-04-30-roll-of-the-dice-a-good-man-is-hard-to-find-pittsburgh.md" >}})" for the very first time, in honor of fallen Sgt. Gerald Vick, who was killed in the line of duty a few days earlier. "[Paradise]({{< relref "2018-01-07-roll-of-the-dice-paradise.md" >}})" is a tour premiere, along with the electric piano on which Bruce plays it.<br>

{{< youtube AjEjoHqz4LA >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2025/05/Star_Tribune_2005_05_11_B5.jpg.webp)

**2006:** The Seeger Sessions Tour moves on to Paris, where Bruce premieres "[Bring 'Em Home]({{< relref "2006-01-18-rotd-cover-me-bring-em-home.md" >}})." The show also features the proper tour premiere of the waltz arrangement of "[If I Should Fall Behind]({{< relref "1992-02-05-roll-of-the-dice-if-i-should-fall-behind.md" >}})." Elliott Murphy and Garland Jeffreys join Bruce for "[Buffalo Gals]({{< relref "2006-01-15-cover-me-roll-of-the-dice-buffalo-gals.md" >}})" in the encore.<br>
