**1972:** The Bruce Springsteen Band headlines at Livingston College in Piscataway, New Jersey.<br>

**1973:** Bruce and his unnamed band play at Seton Hall University.<br>

**1990:** Sting assembles a band for a benefit show for the Rainforest Foundation. The band includes Bruce, Paul Simon, Don Henley, Bruce Hornsby, Herbie Hancock, Brandford Marsalis, Paulinho Da Costa, and Sting. The all-star band meets for a second day of rehearsals in Beverly Hills.<br>

**1997:** During his fourth of five nights at Sydney’s Capitol Theater, Bruce plays “[Freehold]({{< relref "2018-12-03-roll-of-the-dice-freehold.md" >}})” outside of Freehold for the first time.<br>

{{< videopress id=sg2KPvkP >}}

**2002:** Bruce joins Philadelphia band Marah in the studio to play lead guitar and provide background vocals on “[Float Away]({{< relref "2020-08-11-matr-marah-and-bruce-springsteen-float-away.md" >}}).”<br>

{{< youtube cLN9gixVL4U >}}

**2007:** Bruce wins two Grammy Awards: Best Traditional Folk Album (for [*We Shall Overcome: The Seeger Sessions*]({{< relref "2006-01-01-album-companion-we-shall-overcome.md" >}})) and Best Long Form Music Video (for *Wings for Wheels: The Making of Born to Run*)<br>

{{< youtube OG1ctA7FXyU >}}

**2014:** The first night of two in Adelaide kicks off with a party-time cover of “[Summertime Blues]({{< relref "2020-01-02-cover-me-summertime-blues.md" >}})” and an audibled “[Detroit Medley]({{< relref "1976-01-02-cover-me-detroit-medley.md" >}}/).” The show also features a rare outing of “[Back in Your Arms]({{< relref "2019-11-10-roll-of-the-dice-back-in-your-arms.md" >}}).” Also tour premiering tonight: “[My Love Will Not Let You Down]({{< relref "2018-01-24-roll-of-the-dice-my-love-will-not-let-you-down.md" >}}),” “[Human Touch]({{< relref "2019-08-09-roll-of-the-dice-human-touch.md" >}}),” “[Mary’s Place,]({{< relref "2022-03-25-roll-of-the-dice-marys-place.md" >}})” and “[If I Should Fall Behind.]({{< relref "1992-02-05-roll-of-the-dice-if-i-should-fall-behind.md" >}})”<br>
