***New for 2025:** New entry for 2024*<br>

**1973:** Bruce and the E Street Band headline two shows at Max's Kansas City in New York, with The Wailers opening for them.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2019/07/1973-07-19.jpg.webp)

**1978:** Bruce brings the Darkness Tour to Nashville and plays the earliest known performance of "[Factory]({{< relref "2018-10-31-roll-of-the-dice-factory.md" >}})."<br>

**2003:** Night Three of ten at the Rising Tour's Giants Stadium stand features a special guest star: original E Street Band drummer Vini Lopez takes the drums on "[Spirit in the Night]({{< relref "2011-01-08-roll-of-the-dice-spirit-in-the-night.md" >}}/)."<br>

**2009:** A stunner of a Working on a Dream show at the Stadio Olimpico in Turin–Bruce opens with the tour premiere of "[Loose Ends]({{< relref "2022-06-23-roll-of-the-dice-loose-end.md" >}})" and delights with additional tour premieres of "[Drive All Night,]({{< relref "2018-02-27-roll-of-the-dice-drive-all-night.md" >}})" "[Murder Incorporated]({{< relref "1984-01-15-roll-of-the-dice-murder-incorporated.md" >}})," "[My Love Will Not Let You Down]({{< relref "2018-01-24-roll-of-the-dice-my-love-will-not-let-you-down.md" >}})," "Travelin' Band," and "[My Hometown]({{< relref "1983-01-12-roll-of-the-dice-my-hometown.md" >}})."<br>
