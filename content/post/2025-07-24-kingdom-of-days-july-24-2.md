***New for 2025:** New entry for 1973 and 1975; video for 2012*<br>

**1968:** The Castiles play a gig at Le Teendezvous in New Shrewsbury, New Jersey.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/07/1968-0724.jpg.webp)

**1973:** Bruce works on mixing and overdubs for "[Seaside Bar Song]({{< relref "2022-10-24-roll-of-the-dice-seaside-bar-song.md" >}})" and "[Kitty's Back]({{< relref "2022-01-22-roll-of-the-dice-kittys-back.md" >}})" at 914 Sound Studios in Blauvelt, New York.<br>

**1975:** The Born to Run Tour is already underway, but the album *still* isn't finished. Bruce hustles back from his sow in Stockbridge the night before to record a new verse and bridge for "[She's the One]({{< relref "2021-04-04-roll-of-the-dice-shes-the-one.md" >}})" at the Record Plant in New York City.<br>

**1981:** Bruce takes ill and must postpone his scheduled Rosemont show to September.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/07/1981-07-23.jpg.webp)

**1984:** At Night Two in Toronto on the Born in the U.S.A. Tour, Bruce tour premieres "[Trapped]({{< relref "2018-08-05-roll-of-the-dice-cover-me-trapped.md" >}})."<br>

{{< videopress id=ul3sptBK >}}

**1999:** Night Four of fifteen at the Meadowlands at the opening stand of the U.S. Reunion Tour features the tour premiere of "[Point Blank]({{< relref "2020-12-19-roll-of-the-dice-point-blank.md" >}})," its first performance in 14 years, recast in a new contemplative arrangement.<br>

{{< youtube HPdJWh3R5x8 >}}

**2002:** Bruce takes Matt Lauer on a tour of Asbury Park for an upcoming segment of *The Today Show*. They end their tour at Sonny's Southern Cuisine, where Bruce plays "[I've Got a Feeling (Everything's Gonna Be Alright]({{< relref "2020-11-04-matr-the-south-community-choir-of-asbury-park-and-bruce-springsteen-ive-got-a-feeling-everythings-gonna-be-alright.md" >}})" and "My City of Ruins" on his acoustic guitar. The segment will air on July 29th.<br>

{{< youtube W5A6P8r3emk >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/07/2002-07-24.png.webp)

**2003:** Night Four of the ten-night Giants Stadium stand on the Rising Tour features a rare "[Streets of Fire]({{< relref "2021-11-06-roll-of-the-dice-streets-of-fire.md" >}})" in the opening pack.<br>

{{< videopress id=2Mij3jw4 >}}

{{< videopress id=oHoE4Uus >}}

**2005:** Bruce plays a solo acoustic show at the Charlotte Coliseum, one of only two shows on the entire summer leg without at least one tour premiere.<br>

{{< videopress id=1YABZAFn >}}

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/07/2005-07-24.png.webp)

**2012:** Bruce opens Night Two in Bergen with an acoustic "[Long Walk Home]({{< relref "2020-03-21-roll-of-the-dice-long-walk-home.md" >}})" (a Wrecking Ball tour premiere). The show features four additional tour premieres, including the live debut of "[You've Got It]({{< relref "2020-07-30-roll-of-the-dice-youve-got-it.md" >}}/)," the first full-band "[This Depression]({{< relref "2020-02-21-roll-of-the-dice-this-depression.md" >}})," and rare outings for "[My Lucky Day]({{< relref "2019-09-02-roll-of-the-dice-my-lucky-day.md" >}})" and "[Red Headed Woman]({{< relref "2021-09-15-roll-of-the-dice-red-headed-woman.md" >}})."<br>

**2013:** As the Wrecking Ball Tour nears its end, the tour premieres continue to delight and astound. At this show in Leeds, Bruce pulls out back-to-back-to-back premieres of "[Local Hero]({{< relref "1992-02-04-roll-of-the-dice-local-hero.md" >}})," "[Gotta Get That Feeling]({{< relref "2021-02-08-roll-of-the-dice-gotta-get-that-feeling.md" >}})" and "[Bad Moon Rising]({{< relref "2023-01-18-matr-bruce-springsteen-and-john-fogerty-bad-moon-rising.md" >}})," and an ultra-rare "[Secret Garden]({{< relref "2019-05-30-roll-of-the-dice-secret-garden.md" >}})" opens the encores, accompanied by Bruce's heartfelt dedication to travelling fans. This show is available for purchase as part of Bruce's official archive series.
