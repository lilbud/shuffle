**1977:** Bruce and the band play Rochester’s Auditorium Theater, in a show that is available for purchase as part of Bruce’s official archive series.<br>

**2004:** Bruce and the late Warren Zevon win the Grammy Award for Best Rock Vocal Performance by a Duo or Group with Vocal for “[Disorder in the House.]({{< relref "2020-06-08-matr-warren-zevon-and-bruce-springsteen-disorder-in-the-house.md" >}})”<br>

{{< youtube hlUsbtkxRps >}}

**2006:** Bruce wins the Best Solo Rock Vocal Performance Grammy award for “[Devils & Dust]({{< relref "2022-03-02-roll-of-the-dice-devils-dust.md" >}})” and performs the song live at the awards ceremony.<br>

{{< videopress id=MO78meba >}}

“Devils & Dust” was also nominated for Song of the Year, Best Rock song, and Best Long Form Music Video, but it does not win those categories. *Devils & Dust* (the album) is nominated for but does not win the Best Contemporary Folk Album award. Bruce returns to the stage for the finale to perform Wilson Pickett’s “[In the Midnight Hour]({{< relref "2020-12-31-matr-wilson-pickett-and-bruce-springsteen-in-the-midnight-hour.md" >}}).”

**2009:** Bruce takes home the Best Rock Song Grammy Award for “[Girls in Their Summer Clothes]({{< relref "2019-02-17-roll-of-the-dice-girls-in-their-summer-clothes.md" >}}).” The song is also nominated for Best Solo Rock Vocal Performance but does not win.<br>

**2014:** The final night in Perth marks the point where the High Hopes Tour breaks wide open, starting a run of shows with wild, unpredictable covers, tributes to local heroes, and anything goes requests. This show is the first not to feature any songs from *The Rising* since that album debuted, and the show opens with a fantastic cover of AC/DC’s “Highway to Hell.” The encores include the third-ever performance of “[Terry’s Song]({{< relref "2018-07-30-roll-of-the-dice-terrys-song.md" >}}).” Also tour premiering tonight: “[Streets of Fire]({{< relref "2021-11-06-roll-of-the-dice-streets-of-fire.md" >}}),” “[Candy’s Room]({{< relref "2022-05-02-roll-of-the-dice-candys-room.md" >}}),” “[Downbound Train]({{< relref "1984-01-07-roll-of-the-dice-downbound-train.md" >}}),” “[Radio Nowhere]({{< relref "2021-08-01-roll-of-the-dice-radio-nowhere.md" >}}),” “The Promise,” and “[American Land.]({{< relref "2016-01-22-roll-of-the-dice-american-land.md" >}})”<br>
