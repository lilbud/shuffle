**1973:** Bruce continues his five-night stand at The Quiet Knight in Chicago, opening for The Persuasions. Earlier in the day, The New York Daily News reviews his first album.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2020/01/1973-01-25.jpg.webp)

**1990:** At Soundworks West in Los Angeles, Bruce records “[Sad Eyes]({{< relref "2019-10-08-roll-of-the-dice-sad-eyes.md" >}})” with Randy Jackson, David Sancious, Jeff Porcaro, and Michael Fisher. It’ll stay in the vault for eight years before debuting on *Tracks*.<br>

{{< youtube jssnRkHODE0 >}}

**1996:** Bruce plays the Music Hall in Austin, Texas, with Joe Ely joining for a final encore of “[Blowin’ Down This Road]({{< relref "2020-05-27-matr-bruce-springsteen-and-friends-blowin-down-this-road.md" >}}).” After the show, Bruce and Joe catch Willie Nelson’s show nearby.<br>

**2014:** Bruce and the E Street Band continue rehearsing in Cape Town for their upcoming *High Hopes* tour.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/01/2014-0125.jpg.webp)

**2017:** Night Two of three in Perth brings a shift in the show structure as Bruce abandons the chronological set list that characterized the end of the 2016 tour and first night of the 2017 tour, reverting to a more familiar, casual fan-friendly set list.<br>

**2018:** *Springsteen on Broadway* continues its theatrical run at the Walter Kerr Theater in New York City.<br>

**2019:** Bruce and Peter Wolf drop by Mr. Music in Boston and spend about 40 minutes in the music store hanging out and browsing guitars.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/01/2019-01-25.png.webp)

**2023:** Bruce and the E Street Band rehearse for their upcoming tour at Cure Insurance Arena in Trenton.<br>
