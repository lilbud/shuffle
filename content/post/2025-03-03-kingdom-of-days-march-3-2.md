***New for 2025:** Review for 1988 and 1996*<br>

**1973:** Bruce and the band open for Blood, Sweat and Tears in Santa Monica.<br>

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2021/03/1973-03-03.jpg.webp)

![](https://media.githubusercontent.com/media/lilbud/shuffle-archive/refs/heads/master/assets_webp/2018/02/1973-0303.jpg.webp)

**1974:** Bruce and the band play two shows at Georgetown University in Washington, DC. The first show's set list features the entire *Wild & Innocent* album, the last known such occurrence for 35 years. The late show is broadcast on campus radio and features the last performance of "[4th of July, Asbury Park (Sandy)]({{< relref "2019-04-26-roll-of-the-dice-4th-of-july-asbury-park-sandy.md" >}})" with its original lyrics until 2005.<br>
